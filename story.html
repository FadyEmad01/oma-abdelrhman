Our Story

From the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and in the city of Jeddah, Ouma Touch Trading Company was born in 2009 and focused its activity on importing and wholesale selling household utensils, antiques and gifts. It graduated in its activity from scratch . It derived its strength from the trust of its customers until it began in a short period. In manufacturing its ideas, we put our trust in God and set four lines to reach it Praise be to God, the goals have been achieved and we have reached, and we want to be in the first ranks in our activity and to be distributors in the Gulf countries and the Arab and Islamic world.

Our Vision

We look forward to achieving and maintaining a leadership position in our field of business and at the level of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the Gulf and to be distinguished for our customers.

Our Mission
